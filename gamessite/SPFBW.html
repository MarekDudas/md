South Park: The Fractured But Whole

South Park: The Fractured But Whole is a role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft San Francisco and released in 2017. Based on the popular animated TV series "South Park," the game offers a hilarious and satirical take on superhero stories while staying true to the irreverent humor and distinctive art style of the show.

Story

In The Fractured But Whole, players once again assume the role of the New Kid, the silent protagonist from the previous game, South Park: The Stick of Truth. This time, the kids of South Park have shifted their focus from fantasy role-playing to creating their own superhero franchise. As the New Kid, players join Coon and Friends, a group of aspiring child superheroes led by Eric Cartman's alter ego, the Coon.

Game Mechanics

One of the notable features of the game is the emphasis on humor and satire. South Park: The Fractured But Whole satirizes not only the superhero genre but also various aspects of popular culture, politics, and society. The game tackles controversial topics in a comedic and exaggerated manner, making social commentary while delivering a dose of crude humor that fans of the TV series have come to expect.
