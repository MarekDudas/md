Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an immensely popular open-world action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was released in 2013 and has since become one of the best-selling video games of all time.

Story

The game features three main protagonists: Michael De Santa, a retired bank robber living in luxury; Franklin Clinton, a young gangster looking to rise through the ranks; and Trevor Philips, a violent and unpredictable criminal. The trio's stories intertwine as they navigate the criminal underworld and pursue their own ambitions.

Game Mechanics

GTA V also includes an expansive multiplayer mode called Grand Theft Auto Online. Players can create their own customizable character and delve into a persistent online world, where they can complete missions, participate in races, engage in competitive modes, or simply explore the open world with friends. Grand Theft Auto Online has received regular updates, introducing new content, challenges, and events to keep the experience fresh and engaging.
