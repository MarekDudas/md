Battlefield 4

Battlefield 4 is a first-person shooter video game developed by DICE and published by Electronic Arts. Known for its intense multiplayer experience and large-scale warfare, Battlefield 4 takes players into the midst of modern military conflicts.

Story

The game's single-player campaign follows the story of Sergeant Daniel Recker, a member of a U.S. Special Forces squad called "Tombstone." Set in the year 2020, the campaign takes place in various locations around the world, including Azerbaijan, China, and Singapore. The story revolves around a fictional conflict between the United States and Russia, with rising tensions and political turmoil threatening to escalate into a full-scale war.

Game Mechanics

In addition to its compelling single-player campaign, Battlefield 4 is widely known for its robust multiplayer mode. Featuring massive maps, destructible environments, and a wide range of vehicles and weapons, the multiplayer experience in Battlefield 4 offers dynamic and immersive gameplay. Players can engage in various game modes, such as Conquest, Team Deathmatch, and Rush, and collaborate with teammates to achieve victory.

My personal Rating

I remember playing this game on my Xbox 360 when I was 12. The story is very dramatic and packed with action. The multiplayer is perfect, falling buildings, exploding trucks etc. I would highly recommend playing its brothers such as Battlefield 1 and 3.
