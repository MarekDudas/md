Assassin's Creed Rogue

Assassin's Creed Rogue is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft Sofia and released in 2014. Serving as a direct sequel to Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag, the game takes a unique perspective by allowing players to experience the Assassin-Templar conflict from the point of view of a Templar protagonist.

Story

Assassin's Creed Rogue revolves around Shay's personal journey of betrayal and redemption. After a catastrophic event, Shay becomes disillusioned with the Assassin Brotherhood and sets out on a mission to eliminate his former comrades. This unique perspective allows players to witness the conflict from the opposing side, shedding light on the motivations and philosophies of the Templars.

Game Mechanics

One of the standout features of Assassin's Creed Rogue is the naval gameplay, building upon the naval mechanics introduced in Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag. Players can command their own ship, the Morrigan, and engage in naval battles against both Assassins and rival Templars. The harsh Arctic environment presents new challenges, such as icebergs and freezing waters, adding an additional layer of strategy to the naval combat.
