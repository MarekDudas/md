Star Wars Jedi Fallen Order

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an action-adventure game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. Set in the expansive Star Wars universe, the game takes place after the events of Episode III: Revenge of the Sith and follows the story of Cal Kestis, a young Jedi Padawan on a journey of survival and self-discovery.

Story

The game begins with Cal living in hiding after the devastating events of Order 66, which marked the extermination of the Jedi Order. As Cal's true identity is revealed, he is thrust into a dangerous adventure, pursued by the sinister Inquisitors who seek to eliminate any remaining Jedi. With the help of a former Jedi Knight named Cere Junda and a droid named BD-1, Cal sets out on a quest to uncover the secrets of a forgotten civilization and rebuild the Jedi Order.

Game Mechanics

The game features a robust combat system, allowing players to wield a lightsaber with precision and utilize different Force powers to overcome challenges. As Cal progresses, he will unlock new abilities, allowing for more advanced combat techniques and traversal maneuvers. Players must strategically approach encounters, parrying enemy attacks, and using a combination of lightsaber strikes and Force powers to emerge victorious.
